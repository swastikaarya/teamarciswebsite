Our Sponsors

ANSYS has been playing a leading role in the pinnacle of engineering simulation softwares. They help the world’s most innovative companies deliver radically better products to their customers, by providing best in class simulation tools which solve the most complex design challenges limited only by our imagination.

“The 3D experience company” is the world leader in 3D design and Engineering Software. Dassault Systèmes provides PLM & 3D Modeling Software, Simulation Apps and Industry Solutions. They have a wide customer base in industries such as Aerospace & Defense, Architecture, Engineering and Construction, Transport & mobility etc.

Vayavya Labs are industry leaders in Hardware-Software Interface Tools. Vayavya Labs provide embedded software expertise across Automotive, EDA, Semiconductors, and communications verticals. With a large array of services and embedded software, they have had a strong market presence of 12 years, having vast experience and an impressive international clientele.

MATLAB, short for matrix laboratory is a high-performance language for technical computing developed by MathWorks. It integrates computation, visualization, and programming in an easy and convenient environment. Various functions can be performed such as matrix manipulation, function plotting etc. In industry, MATLAB is the tool of choice for high-productivity research, development, and analysis.
